Schoology Learning FAQ

Are Schoology Learning and PowerSchool the same?

PowerSchool (the company) provides a complete suite of industry-leading education solutions. One of those components is Unified
Classroom Schoology Learning®—the leading K-12-focused LMS.

Can we use Schoology Learning with Canvas?

Technically, yes. However, most schools and districts using Schoology Learning have opted to have just one LMS to increase
consistency and productivity throughout their organization.

Can we use Schoology Learning with Google Classroom?

Technically, yes. However, most schools and districts using Schoology Learning have opted to have just one LMS to increase consistency
and productivity throughout their organization. Schoology Learning also provides additional features, including standards
mastery tracking, parent engagement, support for younger learners, and an enhanced overall look and feel of course
layouts that Google Classroom does not support.

Does everyone use Schoology Learning?

Schoology Learning has over 20 million users across 60,000 schools across the globe. To learn more about bringing Schoology Learning
to your school, please request a live demo of our LMS solution.
